DEADLY LEGS

DEADLY LEGS is the ultimate workout for building mass and strength in the legs. This workout is centered around the barbell deadlift, the king of all posterior chain exercises. Not only will you build muscle, but you'll also increase your power and explosiveness. The workout also includes barbell split squats, which will target your legs through a full range of motion, while also providing the added benefits of being a unilateral exercise. This will help improve your balance and stability, making you more athletic. The workout also includes calf raises to give you that complete leg workout. Get ready to unleash the power of your legs with DEADLY LEGS.

LEGENDARY LEGS

Unlock the potential of your legs with LEGENDARY LEGS, where barbell back squats reign supreme as the ultimate leg builder. Supplemented with single leg Romanian deadlifts to hone in on the hamstrings and a special emphasis on the barbell hip thrust for a ballistic booty. Tie up loose ends with calf raises and other exercises, this workout will leave you feeling like a leg-end in the making.
