Leg Workouts

At MAD STRENGTH, we offer two leg workout options:

Powerlifting Legs

This workout focuses on building strength and power in the legs through exercises such as the barbell deadlift, barbell squat, and barbell hip thrust. It also includes exercises for the calves, such as the seated calf raise. This workout is available on Mondays and Thursdays from 6am to 8am.

Athletic Development

This workout focuses on building athleticism and explosiveness in the legs through exercises such as the barbell split squat and standing calf raise. It also includes exercises for the calves, such as the standing calf raise. This workout is available on Tuesdays and Fridays from 6am to 8am.

Both workouts are designed to target all the major muscle groups in the legs and provide a well-rounded and effective workout.
