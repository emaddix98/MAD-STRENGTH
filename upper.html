Upper Body Power

This workout focuses on building strength and power in the upper body through exercises such as the barbell over head press, barbell rows, machine fly, and machine rear delt. It also includes exercises for the triceps and back such as dips and pullups. This workout is available on Wednesdays and Saturdays from 6am to 8am.

Upper Body Definition

This workout focuses on building definition and muscular endurance in the upper body through exercises such as the incline dumbbell press, dumbbell lateral raise, dumbbell one arm row, machine fly, and machine rear delt. It also includes exercises for the triceps and back such as dips and pullups. This workout is available on Sundays and Thursdays from 6am to 8am.
